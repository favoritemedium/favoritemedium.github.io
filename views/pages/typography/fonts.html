Pluto

Pluto Comic Sans walks into a bar and orders a drink. The bartender turns around and says “We don’t serve your type here”. Fortunately, they do serve Pluto, which is a good thing since it is our main typeface. It should be used for main headers and titles. Like a good drink, don’t over do it. Use Pluto when you really want to draw attention. There are times when Pluto will not be available, for example in some of our Google Docs. If Pluto is not available then use Helvetica Neue instead.

Open Sans

Open Sans should be used for everything else. Body copy, captions, instructions messages and probably most labels should all be in Open Sans. Elements like buttons could be in Pluto or Open Sans depending on the importance of the button. Yah! We love free Google Fonts.
